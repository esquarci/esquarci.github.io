About

Part researcher, part designer, part product thinker.

I'm Elisabetta — a UX-focused product professional with 4+ years across product management, product ownership and growth.

My path into product was a winding one. I studied Biochemistry at Bristol, grew up bilingual between Italy and Switzerland, and came to digital products through an e-commerce startup where I ended up touching almost every part of the business — marketing, operations, user research, and eventually product.

That range taught me something I still lean on: the best product work happens when you understand the whole system around a screen, not just the screen itself. Since then I've worked at Aviva optimising customer journeys on a complex insurance platform, and at Hyper Studio supporting 80+ early-stage teams shape their first versions.

Right now I'm completing a Professional Diploma in UX Design at the UX Design Institute, deepening the craft side — particularly research, evidence-based design, and experimentation. I'm available for new roles from 2026.
